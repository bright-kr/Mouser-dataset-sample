# # Mouser-dataset-samples

<h2>A sample dataset of 1001 Mouser products</h2>

![Mouser dataset header](https://github.com/luminati-io/Google-Shopping-dataset-sample/blob/main/Google-Shopping-datasets.png)

A Mouser dataset sample of over 1000 records. Dataset was extracted using the <b>Bright Data API</b>.

<h2>Some of the data points that are included in the Mouser dataset:</h2>

* ```product_url```: URL pointing to the detailed page of the product  
* ```category_url```: URL pointing to the category page of the product  
* ```mouser_part_num```: Mouser's unique identifier for the product  
* ```mfr_part_number```: Manufacturer's part number for the product  
* ```manufacturer```: The name of the manufacturer of the product  
* ```image```: URL or identifier of the product image  
* ```image_high```: Higher resolution image URL or identifier  
* ```manufacturer_url```: URL pointing to the manufacturer's website  
* ```datasheet_url```: URL pointing to the datasheet for the product  
* ```rohs_compliant```: Indicates whether the product is RoHS compliant  
* ```rohs_compliant_url```: URL providing details about RoHS compliance  
* ```factory_lead_time```: Lead time for factory production  
* ```description```: Detailed description of the product  
* ```availability```: Availability status of the product  
* ```qty_min```: Minimum order quantity  
* ```qty_mult```: Order quantity increment  
* ```qty_reel```: Quantity per reel  
* ```quantity_available```: Current available quantity  
* ```q_minimum```: Minimum quantity for order  
* ```coil_current```: Current flowing through the coil  
* ```breadcrumbs```: Breadcrumb trail indicating the product's location on the website  
* ```actuator_length```: Length of the actuator  
* ```actuator_style```: Style or design of the actuator  
* ```actuator```: Component responsible for producing motion  
* ```airflow```: Airflow characteristics  
* ```application```: Common applications or uses  
* ```bearing_type```: Type of bearing used  
* ```brand```: Brand name associated with the product  

And a lot more.

This is a sample subset which is derived from the "Mouser"

<h2>What are the Mouser datasets use cases?</h2>

<h3>1. Identify Suppliers</h3>
Identify trusted suppliers and high-demand components on Mouser to streamline sourcing processes and enhance product quality.

<h3>2. Analyze Trends</h3>
Analyze market trends, pricing patterns, and product demand on Mouser to strategically position your offerings, ensuring competitive pricing and maximizing market appeal.

<h3>3. Track Demand</h3>
Monitor demand trends on Mouser to optimize inventory management, maintain ideal stock levels, and minimize issues such as overstocking or stockouts.
